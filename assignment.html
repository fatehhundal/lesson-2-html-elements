St. Stephen's School, Carramar Campus (K-12)

50 St Stephen's Cres, Tapping WA 6065

There are about 400 people in St. Stephen's School's secondary school area (Years 7-12).

The school offers courses for Arts, English and Languages, Health and Physical Education, Humanities, Mathematics, Science, Technologies, and Biblical Studies.

The school is registered to the School Curriculum and Standards Authority (SCSA), which is responsible for the school curriculum and standards in Western Australia.
